# PAMGuard Help

Welcome to the PAMGuard FX help system. Use the navigation panel on the left to
browse help topics for individual modules.

## Modules covered in this help

### Utilities
- [Database](database.md) – Store PAMGuard data in SQLite or MySQL
- [Binary Storage](binary_storage.md) – Store detector output in binary files

### Sound Processing
- [Sound Acquisition](sound_acquisition.md) – Read live or recorded audio
- [Sound Output](sound_output.md) – Play back audio through a sound card
- [FFT Engine](fft_engine.md) – Generate spectrogram data
- [Filters](filters.md) – IIR and FIR digital filters
- [Decimator](decimator.md) – Reduce sample rate of audio data
- [Noise Band Monitor](noise_band_monitor.md) – Measure noise levels in frequency bands

### Detectors
- [Click Detector](click_detector.md) – Detect transient echolocation clicks
- [Click Train Detector](click_train_detector.md) – Detect repeating patterns of clicks
- [Whistle & Moan Detector](whistle_moan_detector.md) – Detect tonal vocalisations
- [CPOD Importer](cpod_importer.md) – Import CPOD/FPOD click data

### Classifiers
- [Matched Template Classifier](matched_template_classifier.md) – Classify clicks by template matching
- [Deep Learning Classifier](deep_learning_classifier.md) – Deploy deep learning models in PAMGuard
